An unprecedented 70.8 million people have been forcibly displaced worldwide. 37,000 thousand people are forced to flee their homes everyday due to conflict or persecution

Refugee Stories is a platform that aims to provide the most vulnerable among us a space to share their stories. Our goal is to help people visiting the site to gain a better understanding of the refugee crisis and what it means to be a refugee.
